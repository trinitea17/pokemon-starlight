# Pokemon Starlight

Welcome to the repository of Pokemon Starlight!

Alpha coming soon!

Pokemon Starlight is a Rom-Hack based on Pokemon Emerald, adventuring through the region of Hisale!

# Adventure Through Hisale!
Pokemon Starlight is intended to be a more casual Pokemon experience, akin to older pokemon games. As a new trainer in Hisale, you leave home to embark on a Pokemon adventure! Fight the 8 Gyms in Hisale, battle a mysterious organisation and fill up your Pokedex with the new pokemon found in the world.

# FEATURES
* Explore a Whole New Region: Hisale is yours to explore, with new Pokemon to find, new characters, locations and stories to uncover.
* Handpicked Pokédex: Catch and train from a curated mix of Pokémon across generations—balanced, diverse, and full of surprises. Including all new Pokemon to find and fresh takes on old Pokemon!
* Mega Evolution: Uncover the Mega Stones in the world and take your Pokemon beyond their capabilities! And who knows... you may find some new mega stones along the way...
